Torrent websites for TV Shows like YIFY (2021)

Almost everyone knows YIFY (or YTS) and what it offers: movie torrents, low size, easy to use, without costs. But what if I don't want movies, but I want to get TV Shows. Well, here we review some alternatives to Yts for TV series.

EZTV

It is the first alternative that comes to one's head thinking about YIFY. It is the source of TV shows used by the old PopcornTime. The downside is that the interface is uncomfortable and some content is missing. For example there are no episodes of "Friends".
Go to EZTV

RARBG

This site is more unspecific than the previous one, but it must be on the list. It has both TV shows and movies, in addition to other torrents. It is organized and somewhat more comfortable to use than Eztv. One difference from Eztv is that it also contains season packs, which makes it very convenient to download various files from TV shows. Also, these packages are the best way to find old content on the P2P network, Ex: "Friends".
Go to RARBG

YSeries

A more enjoyable site, with a mobile interface. Like EZTV it is specific to TV shows. Compared to RARBG it has a similar organization, also containing packs. It is not my usefulness, but also you can watch TV shows online. It is a more comfortable place to decide which TV shows to download before starting to make the popcorn. Go to YSeries

Others sites

There are other sites that offer torrents, but are less specific or impractical for TV series: 1337x, ExtraTorrents, Torrentz, kat.sx, etc.

Conclusion

We reviewed three TV show download sites similar to YIFY, all of them useful and easy to use. Regarding content, in situations it is common to use one or the other, if the required content is not recent, Eztv is of little use. RARBG and YSeries have the greatest variety in quality and size. The final recommendation is that you add these three sites to your bookmarks and do not miss any part of your favorite TV shows.
